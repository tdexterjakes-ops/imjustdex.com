II

Origin

2 AM / Dallas

It was 2 AM.

A revision loop on a campaign that should have taken twenty minutes.

The copy was fine. Technically.

It just didn't sound like us. It sounded like every other church. Every other flyer. Every other Instagram caption written by a well-meaning volunteer at 11:47 PM on a Saturday.

I kept rewriting the same three sentences. Not because the words were wrong. Because the voice was wrong. I had taste. Taste doesn't scale.

You can't enforce what you haven't named.

You can't delegate what you haven't documented.

You can't scale what only lives in your head.

So I built one. For my house first. Then I productized it. This page is the foundation. Free. Permanent. Yours.
