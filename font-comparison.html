VERDICT

The Move Is Instrument Serif

Arial Narrow reads as anemic — system-default, condensed, no editorial weight. It collapses under Impact instead of standing next to it.

Instrument Serif brings the friction. The high-contrast italic against blunt Impact display is the brutalist editorial pairing — magazine bones, refined finish.

Fraunces is the safer serif play — more range, more traditional. Use this if Instrument feels too stylized at body sizes.

IBM Plex Sans is the strongest sans alternative — geometric, weighted, brutalist-coded. The fallback if you want to stay in the sans family.
